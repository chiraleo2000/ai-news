## Summary

The Trump administration issued an export control order requiring Anthropic to pull its two most powerful models — Fable 5 and Mythos 5 — offline due to vague 'national security concerns.' The directive was triggered after Amazon CEO Andy Jassy reportedly informed the White House that researchers had found a way to bypass Fable 5's safety guardrails. Anthropic, lacking the ability to verify users' nationality, took the models entirely offline. Cybersecurity experts have signed an open letter asking Trump to revoke the order, arguing the ban endangers network defenders in the U.S. Analysts note the crackdown may paradoxically benefit Anthropic by giving its models a reputation as the most powerful and dangerous — with some users eager to access what the government fears most.
