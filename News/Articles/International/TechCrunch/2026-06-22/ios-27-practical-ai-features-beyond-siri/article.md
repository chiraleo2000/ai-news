## Summary

While Apple's revamped Siri grabbed headlines at WWDC 2026, iOS 27 delivers a suite of smaller but highly practical AI features woven into existing apps. Apple Intelligence powers bill splitting via photo of a restaurant receipt, automated password upgrades for compromised credentials, one-tap contextual suggestions in Messages (add to calendar, find photos, set reminders), Call Context that surfaces reservation codes during customer service calls, and natural language Shortcuts creation. Safari gains AI-organized tab groups based on browsing context. The Home app consolidates related smart home events into single notifications. All features operate on-device for privacy, with the public beta arriving soon and general release expected in fall 2026. Apple's strategy emphasizes making software feel smarter rather than pushing users toward explicit chatbot interactions.
