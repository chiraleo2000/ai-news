## Summary

John Jumper, the Nobel Prize laureate in Chemistry who co-created AlphaFold at Google DeepMind — the landmark protein structure prediction system — is leaving DeepMind to join rival AI safety company Anthropic. The move is a notable talent win for Anthropic, which has been under pressure following the Trump administration's export control order targeting its Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models. Jumper's expertise in biological AI and scientific applications would significantly strengthen Anthropic's research capabilities. His departure represents a high-profile talent loss for DeepMind and signals Anthropic's continued ability to attract world-class scientific talent despite ongoing regulatory turbulence.
