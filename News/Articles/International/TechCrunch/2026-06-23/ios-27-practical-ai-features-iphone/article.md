## Summary

iOS 27 brings a wave of practical Apple Intelligence features beyond the overhauled Siri: AI bill splitting, automated password updates, one-tap message suggestions, call context, vibe-coded Shortcuts, intelligent Home notifications, and Safari tab organization.

---

## Body

Apple Intelligence in iOS 27 embeds AI across native apps rather than concentrating it in a single chatbot. All features run on-device for privacy.

**Bill Splitting:** Take a photo of a restaurant receipt; Apple Intelligence extracts items, quantities, tip, and total. Share requests via Messages; pay via Apple Cash double-click.

**AI Password Update (Agentic):** Identifies weak and breached passwords, then autonomously navigates websites to change them — no manual action required.

**One-tap Messages Suggestions:** Context-aware suggestions in iMessage — add a request to Reminders, share relevant Photos from library, create a Calendar event — as single-tap actions.

**Call Context:** Surfaces relevant info from Mail (e.g., airline confirmation codes) on the call screen when calling businesses. On-device, private.

**Vibe Coding Shortcuts:** Describe automations in natural language — "text partner my ETA when I leave work," "turn porch lights on when DoorDash arrives."

**Home App Intelligence:** Groups multiple smart home events into single notifications.

**Safari Tab Organizer:** Automatically groups open tabs by topic, displayed above the webpage.

Features are live in developer beta; public release fall 2026.
