## Summary

## Body

OpenAI is expanding Daybreak to democratize patching at machine speed. Key announcements:

**Codex Security (updated plugin):** Full defensive workflows — deep scans, attack path tracing, threat model generation, patch creation, SARIF/CodeQL export. Stats so far: 30,000+ repos scanned, 30M+ commits analyzed, 500,000+ findings auto-fixed.

**GPT-5.5-Cyber (full release):** Now available to verified defenders after limited preview.
- 85.6% CyberGym (vs 81.8% GPT-5.5)
- 39.5% ExploitGym (vs 25.95% GPT-5.5)
- 69.8% SEC-bench Pro (vs 63.1% GPT-5.5)

**Daybreak Cyber Partner Program:** 28 security companies integrate GPT-5.5 with Trusted Access for Cyber, including: Accenture, Akamai, CrowdStrike, Cisco, Cloudflare, Darktrace, Elastic, Fortinet, IBM, Okta, Palo Alto Networks, Proofpoint, SentinelOne, Sophos, Tenable, Trend AI, Wiz, Zscaler.

**Government Trusted Access partnerships:** Australia, Canada, France, Germany, Japan, South Korea, ENISA (EU).

**Patch the Planet:** Open-source initiative with Trail of Bits and HackerOne.
