## Body

OpenEnv is an open-source environment framework designed specifically for training and evaluating AI agents using reinforcement learning (agentic RL). It gathered 83 upvotes on launch with support from 14+ contributing organizations.

**Key features:**
- Standardized open-source environments for agentic RL training
- Focuses on long-horizon tasks, not just single-step RL benchmarks
- Community-driven development model on Hugging Face
- Backed by 14+ academic and industry organizations

**Context:** The launch comes as major AI labs rely increasingly on RL (RLHF, RLAIF, process reward models) to develop capable agents. Open-source RL environments lower the barrier for academic researchers and smaller teams to contribute to this critical area.

OpenEnv fills a notable gap — while there are many LLM benchmarks, standardized open-source environments for training and evaluating autonomous agents on multi-step tasks have been limited.
